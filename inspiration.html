Game that inspired Darksouls

Although the game didn't have a direct inspiration, the game Demon's Souls developed by FromSoftware and directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki the maker of Darksouls introduced the core principles such as the combat, death mechanics, multiplayer, storytelling and dark fantasy setting that would be copied by the Dark Souls series

There is alot of games that were inspired by Dark souls some of them are:

Elden Ring

bloodborne

Blasphemous

Helpoint
